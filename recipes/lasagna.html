Lasagna

This is a very tasty lasagna that is easy to make

Lasagna is one of those meals that you never really cook because it is terrible for you. Maybe you should though, because nobody lives forever.

Don't overpay on most of the ingredients. The Sauce really overwhelms everything else you put in there, so just focus on that.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Make the Sauce from the Tomatoes
  3. Top with Cheese
